Hello! My name is Vsevolod I Front-end developer.

I started with Basic at school and Pascal at the University. I studied C ++ back in 2001. I tried to create applications for Android. I ordered my first online store on Joomla in a web-studio in 2008. Then I myself began to make websites on the designers and ready-made CMS templates. I had to learn more about HTML, CSS and JS.

I liked it, and I realized that this is what I want to devote all of myself! In 2018, I systematized my knowledge and took two 4-month courses "Frontend" and "Backend". Since then, no day passes without a line of code.

Now I am looking for teamwork and are open to suggestions.
